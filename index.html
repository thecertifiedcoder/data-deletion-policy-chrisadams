Account & Data Deletion Policy

If you wish to delete your account and associated data, please follow these steps:

  1. Log in to your account in the app.
  2. Navigate to the More tab in the bottom navigation.
  3. Find and tap the Delete Your Account button.
  4. Follow the on-screen instructions to confirm deletion.
  5. For more details, visit our official policy page: View Data Deletion Policy
